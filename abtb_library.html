New Benefit for ABTB Members!

Anybody can attend Free ABTB Movie Nights (watch the bulletin board!) but you must be a dues-paying club member to borrow DVDs.

Any ABTB member in good standing can borrow movies from the club library at no cost. An AA-style donation of $1 per person per hour is suggested but not required. For example, a two-hour movie for two people suggests that you leave $4 in the DVD case when you return it.

Money from ABTB memberships helps us cover operating costs and building improvements. 50% of movie donations go to the Chuck B. memorial Building Fund. One brick at a time! In the meantime, the money’s in ABTB’s bank account in case someday Westend Group falls on hard times and can’t make the rent.

ABTB members have donated these DVDs to the club, and all of the movies are related to alcohol or substance abuse in some way. AA doesn’t "approve" or "sanction" movies, and Alano By The Bay does not recommend any particular film. Some movies may be disturbing to people in recovery (strong triggers, realistic depictions, negative outcomes, etc.).

To reserve a movie, send an email to webmaster@alanobythebay.org with the name of the movie, when you would like it, and how long you want to borrow it (usually 1 week).

Your ABTB membership fee is your security deposit. If you don't return an item in good condition we'll deduct the cost of a replacement, shipping, and tax, plus a $10 penalty, and we reserve the right to harbor a resentment.
